Each year more New Yorkers have discovered that getting onto the water in small boats is exhilerating and relaxing, a time for quiet reflection or challenging exertion.


As we learn more about boating, we realize that knowing about the waterways is the best way to stay safe. Many of us use APPS to make preparations easier.


APPS for Boaters is a website to showcase the best of these APPS, to provide an opportunity for boaters to exchange information, rate APPS, and make recommendations for others.


We hope you enjoy reading about the APPS we have chosen, and talking about your favorites too!
