APPS for Boaters

Previous Next

Beginner, intermediate or advanced, enjoyable boating depends on understanding maritime conditions -- by direct observation, talking to experienced others, and online learning. The U.S. Coast Guard requires float plans with destinations, times of departure and return, and info re who is on board. Boaters must show a marine weather forecast along with local conditions including tides, current, and wind.

Click below for the best APPs to answer these questions and more!

NewBees

You are now beginning to experience small boating on the rivers and harbor of NYC, it can be scary at first. Friends will ask "Isn't it dangerous?" or "If the river flows both ways, how do you know where to go?" and etc. These APPs will answer your questions, and keep you going as you begin to learn why people love NYC boating so much...
