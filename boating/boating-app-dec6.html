APPS for Boaters

Previous Next

Safe and enjoyable boating depends on boaters understanding local maritime conditions, and this knowledge is gained in many ways - by direct observation, by talking to others with experience, and by online learning. The U.S. Coast Guard requires boaters to prepare float plans detailing destinations, approximate times of departure and return, plus info re who is on board. In addition boaters must list a marine weather forecast along with actual conditions of tides, current, and wind.

Here is your source for the APPs that can make it happen!
